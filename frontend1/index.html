Our Services

Workflow

Collaborating with Customer, feedback is a must to make the best design

01
Workflow

Introducing the team to customer for small briefing A warm greet is a must for building a good chemistry

02
Approachment

Site visit : measuring site, gathering datas (nature, customer needs, habits, function, preference, etc), more briefing

03
Suggestion

Making concept design by gathering the datas from the approach step and making mockup (3D illustration)

04
Adjustment

Present to customer the concept design for getting feedback and doing some revision from it

05
Final Design

Deliver the final design to customer along with complementory documents

06
Supervising

We helps client in supervising the progress of build-up the project if is needed
